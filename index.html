Lemon Rice

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon split urad dal
  • 1-2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander, chopped

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds and urad dal.
  2. Once the seeds crackle, add green chilies and turmeric powder.
  3. Add cooked rice to the pan, mix well, and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Turn off the heat, add lemon juice, and mix well.
  5. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve.

Coffee

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon coffee powder
  • 1-2 teaspoons sugar (to taste)
  • Milk or cream (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Boil water in a pot.
  2. Add coffee powder and let it brew for a few minutes.
  3. Add sugar and stir.
  4. Strain the coffee into a cup and add milk or cream if desired.
  5. Serve hot.
